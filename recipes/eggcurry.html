Egg Curry Recipe

Description

Egg curry is a delicious and spicy dish made with boiled eggs simmered in a flavorful tomato and onion gravy. It's a popular dish in Indian cuisine.

This is a quick solution to a curry when there is no meat or fish. Best eaten hot over white rice. Garnish with cilantro leaves.

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Cut boiled eggs in half and add them to the gravy. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with cilantro.

Notes

You can adjust the spice level by adding more or fewer green chilies. Serve with rice or naan for a complete meal.
