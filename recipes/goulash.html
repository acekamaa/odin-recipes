Goulash Supreme Recipe

Description

Goulash is a hearty stew of meat and vegetables, seasoned with paprika and other spices. It is a traditional dish in many Central European countries.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the bell pepper, tomatoes, broth, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with bread or over rice.

Notes

Feel free to add other vegetables like carrots or potatoes for extra flavor and nutrition.

Serving Suggestions

Goulash is best served with crusty bread or over a bed of rice. You can also top it with sour cream or shredded cheese for added richness.

Variations

Try adding different spices or herbs to customize the flavor. You can also use different types of meat, such as pork or chicken, or make it vegetarian by using beans and lentils instead of meat.
