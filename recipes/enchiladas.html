Chicken Enchiladas I - Mexican dish

Description

This chicken enchilada recipe is easy to make with tortillas, tender chicken, and a deliciously cheesy sauce for a family-pleasing Mexican meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with half of the enchilada sauce and half of the cheese.
  3. Fill each tortilla with the chicken mixture, roll them up, and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top and sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Notes

You can add black beans, corn, or bell peppers to the chicken mixture for extra flavor and nutrition.
